Tasty Recipe for Shepherd's Pie

Description

Easy Shepherd's Pie recipe for a quick dinner for the family or friends. Packed with veggies and meat, topped with a creamy mix of potatoes and cheese. Follow along this easy recipe!

Ingredients

  • 4 Potatoes
  • 2 Tbsp butter
  • 3 Tbs milk
  • Salt & Pepper
  • 1 Diced Onion
  • 1 lb. Ground Beef
  • Shredded cheese
  • 6oz of Tomato sauce

Steps

  1. Pre-heat oven to 350 degrees
  2. Peel, dice and boil potatoes
  3. Saute pan over medium heat and saute onion and beef
  4. Add mixture to baking dish
  5. Top with mashed potatoes
  6. Bake for 20 minutes
  7. Add cheese and bake for another 5-10 minutes
