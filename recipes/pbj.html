Recipe for PBJ

Description

Recipe for a classic peanut butter and jelly sandwich. No frills. If it ain't broke, don't fix it. Am I right?!

Ingredients

  • 2 Slices of Bread
  • Peanut butter
  • Grape jam (or any fruit jelly you prefer)

Steps

  1. Take a knife and apply peanut butter to one piece of bread
  2. Apply jelly to the other piece of bread
  3. Put the two pieces together
    • Slice off crust (optional)
  4. Enjoy!
