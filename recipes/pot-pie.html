Tasty Recipe for Vegetable Pot Pie

Description

This is a recipe for Vegetable Pot Pie. This dish is perfect for small and large gatherings and this recipe serves 8-10 people. Double the recipe for bigger parties.

There is also a vegan version for those who do not want to use any dairy or may be allergic.

Ingredients Needed:

  • Pre-made pie dough
  • 16oz Cream of mushroom
  • 5 Carrots
  • 1 Celery
  • 1 Onions
  • Salt/Pepper
  • 3 Potatoes
  • Shallots (optional)

Recipe Steps:

  1. Pre-heat oven to 400 degrees.
  2. Dice all vegetables
  3. Sautee Vegetables
  4. Add Cream of Mushroom to Vegetable mix
  5. Place vegetables in baking tray
  6. Place pie dough over vegetable mix in baking tray
  7. Cook for 45 minutes or until crust is golden brown
